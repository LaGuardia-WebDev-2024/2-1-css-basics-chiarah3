The coolest creature: The Red Panda

The Red Panda is awesome because...I mean, look at it. Red Panda's are adorable, and are accustomed to sleeping for long lengths during the day in trees. They're social with each other, and as you can see, very fluffy, and very red. They are very distant relatives of the Panda bear, despite it's name, and share more genetic similarities with weasles, skunks, and (as one can probably tell from their appearence) racoons.
